savoury
```
  Savoury food is salty or spicy and not sweet in taste
  -- 咸的；辛辣的

  -- 令人愉快的；名声好的
```

favour
```
  U the support or approval of something or someone
  -- 支持，赞同
  >> be in favour of sth/doing sth - to support or approve of something.
  >> -- 赞同做
  >> in one's favour - when something is in your favour, it gives you an advantage.
  >> -- 对..有利
  C a kind action that you do for someone
  -- 帮助，善意行为，恩惠
```
